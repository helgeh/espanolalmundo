What we are all about

What we offer

Leonlingua is a Spanish school located in León, Nicaragua. We give you a possibility to practice three hours a week with a teacher who is native speaker, and who will communicate with you only in Spanish.

How it works

You can reserve the hours that suits you best according to your own timetable. We offer you the first lesson for free!

Video conferences

In a video conference with a teacher and other students it is almost like in a class room. By using familiar technologies like Skype and Google Hangouts the student connects with a native speaking Spanish teacher.

How to start

Contact us for an offer. We can test your knowledge to be sure your teacher will know where to start.

Educated teachers

Our teachers are highly qualified and are specialized in teaching Spanish as a foreign language. We provide language classes for all levels, A1- C2.

For how long

We recommend that you sign up for at least six months. Then you will see results and feel more confident in your learning process. Try for a week at first and you will be an annual subscriber before you know it!
